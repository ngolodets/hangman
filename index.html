Game Instructions:

A player must select a letter of the alphabet. If the letter is contained in the word, take another turn guessing a letter. To reveal a letter, type it into the box, which will then trigger the letter to be revealed, if it is contained in the word. If the letter is not contained in the word, click the Try Again button, and a portion of the hangman will be added. The game continues until either the word is guessed or all the parts of the hangman are displayed - you have 10 tries. Good luck!
